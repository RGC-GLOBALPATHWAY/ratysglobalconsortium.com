Our Integrated Framework

Three Pillars.
One Ecosystem.

Each pillar of RGC Global Pathway addresses a critical gap between education and employment — together forming a complete, scalable student development platform powered by Raty's Global Consortium.

01
TalentBridge

Identifies, validates, and showcases student potential beyond academics — surfacing communication, leadership, creativity, and problem-solving through institution-endorsed visibility platforms.

02
SkillLab

Delivers structured employability and future-skills programs — communication, aptitude, professional, and digital competencies aligned with global industry expectations and outcome-based education principles.

03
TalentOS

The outcomes and intelligence layer — consolidating participation, skill acquisition, and readiness indicators into validated profiles and institutional reports that support placement planning and quality assurance.

About RGC Global Pathway

A Strategic Partner,
Not a Vendor.

RGC Global Pathway, powered by Raty's Global Consortium, was founded in 2019 with a clear purpose — to enable institutions to move from academic delivery to measurable student outcomes that meet evolving global workforce demands.

Who We Are

International Education & Talent
Development Consortium

RGC Global Pathway, powered by Raty's Global Consortium, partners with higher-education institutions to strengthen student employability, talent visibility, and placement readiness in an increasingly competitive global environment.

We operate as a strategic partner — working alongside institutional leadership, faculty, and placement teams to deliver measurable student development, improved placement preparedness, and enhanced institutional credibility.

"Bridging campus readiness with workforce demand through structured placement and talent preparation."
